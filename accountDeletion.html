How To Delete Your Data On Our App?




We only collect data fore registered accounts - if you have registered in the past, and want your data deleted, check the instructions below:


  1. Tap The "Settings" Button
  2. Choose "Profile"
  3. Log into your account
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Type "CONFIRM" in order to permanently delete your account
  6. Done! All of your data is permanently deleted from our servers!
